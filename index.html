✿ ABOUT ME

Hey! I'm Chet, a Computer Science student at UNC Chapel Hill, with a passion for turning ideas into playable experiences. Whether it's building a Pokémon-style RPG from scratch or generating infinite fantasy worlds, I love projects that live at the intersection of code and creativity.

Outside of coding you'll find me deep in a video game, hunting for new music, or keeping up with UNC sports or the Toronto Maple Leafs.

🎮 GAMING

Video games are what got me into programming in the first place. I grew up wanting to understand how the worlds I was exploring actually worked — and eventually started building my own.

My taste is broad: from old-school JRPGs like EarthBound and Pokémon to modern indie titles. If it has good world-building, I'm in.

Game design is where my passion for software really lives — building systems that feel fun to interact with, not just functional.

🎵 MUSIC

Music is a constant for me — on while coding, on while gaming, on while doing pretty much anything.

My taste covers a lot of ground. I'm always hunting for something new to put on, and I genuinely enjoy the process of stumbling onto an artist or album I'd never heard before.

Video game soundtracks count too — Mason Lindroth and Hiroshi Kanazu live rent-free in my head.

🏒 SPORTS

Big sports household growing up, and that stuck. I follow a few teams closely and will watch almost any sport if the stakes are right.

On the college side, it's all about the UNC Tar Heels — basketball especially. Dean Smith Court is a special place.

And then there's the Toronto Maple Leafs, who are contractually obligated to break my heart every spring.
